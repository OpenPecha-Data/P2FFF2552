III. Dharmamūla:

atha khalv āyuṣmāñ śāradvatīputro ’kṣayamatiṃ bodhisattvam idam avocat:kasmāt, kulaputra, tava
nāmākṣayamatir iti prajñaptam? akṣayamatir āha: ayaṃ, bhadanta śāradvatīputrākṣayamatir
iti sarvadharmārambaṇaniṣyandaḥ. tat kasya hetoḥ? sarvadharmākṣayatvāt.āha: pratibhātu te,
kulaputra, bodhisattvānāṃ dharmākṣayatānirdeśam ārabhya;
